DESIGN RATIONALE

Color Fusion

Option 5's dark base shifted from blue-black to green-black (#080e0a). Dual accent system: green (#4a9a54) from O1 for organic elements + gold (#c9a84c) from O5 for celestial/precious elements. Together they feel like a moonlit garden.

Typography Blend

Cinzel (O5) for display headers. Lora (O1) for card titles. EB Garamond (O5) for body reading. Caveat (O1) for dates, subtitles, and handwritten accents. Best of both typographic worlds.

Botanical Constellations

O5's constellation lines become vine-like curves (quadratic beziers) instead of straight lines. Star dots get tiny leaf shapes beside them. Background is half starfield, half dark forest canopy.

Card Design

O1's dashed borders and botanical emoji decorations on O5's dark cards. Hover turns dashed → solid (organic → crystallized). Rune badge from O5 in top-right. Handwritten dates from O1.

Editor — Notion-style

Block-based editing: every paragraph, heading, quote, and callout is a discrete block with hover-visible drag handles (⠿) and + buttons. Floating toolbar appears on text selection instead of a fixed toolbar. Slash commands (/) for inserting new block types. Inline page properties replace the old meta panel at the bottom.

The Menu

System items (Settings, Analytics, AI Bot) moved from the sidebar nav into a bottom user menu popup. Click the user avatar to reveal a floating menu with settings, shortcuts, and account actions — keeping the sidebar focused purely on content navigation.
