New Entry

There's something about watching seeds push through dark earth that teaches you what no book can. Patience isn't passive — it's the active decision to keep watering when you can't see results.

The garden doesn't care about your timeline. It grows when it's ready. And yet, every morning you show up, you check the soil, you pull the weeds. That consistency isn't glamorous, but it's the only thing that works.

I think writing is the same way. You plant words in rows, you tend them, and sometimes — if the light is right — something blooms that surprises even you.

Design Rationale

Color Philosophy

Moves from pure dark to warm parchment (#faf6ef). Green accent (#3a7d44) represents growth and life. Gold (#b8923e) for metadata adds an aged manuscript feel.

Typography

Lora for headings/body (warm serif), DM Sans for UI elements (clean sans), Caveat for handwritten accents (dates, taglines).

Layout Shift

Replaces dense grid with generous whitespace. Cards have rounded corners and dashed borders. Botanical emoji as decorative elements replace runes.

Editor Changes

Light mode editor with the same warm palette. Drop cap feature for first paragraphs. Handwritten font for subtitles and dates. Softer, rounder toolbar buttons.

Key Departure

Completely inverts the dark mystical aesthetic. Aims for "sun-drenched notebook left open in a meadow." Approachable rather than mysterious.

Interaction

Hover effects are gentle lifts with green glow. No flickering animations. Cards feel tactile like paper. Transitions are slow and organic (0.3s ease).
